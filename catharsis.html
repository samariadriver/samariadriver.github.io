About Catharsis

Catharsis means the release of negative toxins. Catharsis EP is about letting go negativity and embracing positivity. The album cover (my profile pic)communicates the message of the album perfectly. The red symbolizes the passion I had when I wrote the songs which I hope is contagious to the listener. The water falling off the petals symbolize change, which is what I hope the listeners can do as they progress through the EP and become a better version of themselves. Lastly, the rose is blossoming and becoming more beautiful on the inside then out; this was my intent for writing this EP. Enjoy!
